> Most home networks are "flat." This means your smart lightbulb, your work laptop, and your personal gaming rig are all talking to each other on the same digital floor. From a security standpoint, this is a nightmare.
>
> **The Solution: VLANs (Virtual Local Area Networks)**\
> By implementing VLAN segmentation, we divide one physical router into multiple isolated virtual networks.
>
> **The CTS Security Blueprint:**
>
> 1. **IoT Isolation:** Putting "dumb" devices (cameras, smart plugs) on a network that can reach the internet but cannot touch your personal files.
> 2. **The DMZ:** A dedicated zone for hosting servers or FiveM roleplay environments, ensuring that if a guest enters your server, they can't enter your PC.
> 3. **The Secure Core:** A hardened VLAN reserved exclusively for financial transactions and sensitive data.
>
> Hardening your network doesn't require enterprise-level hardware. With the right configuration, your existing infrastructure can be turned into a fortress.
