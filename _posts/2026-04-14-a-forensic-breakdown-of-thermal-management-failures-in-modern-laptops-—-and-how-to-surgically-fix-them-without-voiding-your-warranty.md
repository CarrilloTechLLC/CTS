Have you ever noticed your gaming laptop or workstation slowing down exactly when the action gets intense? You aren’t imagining it; your hardware is intentionally sabotaging itself to survive. This is **Thermal Throttling.**

Modern processors like the Intel i7 or Ryzen 9 are engineering marvels, but they generate immense heat in small chassis. When the CPU hits its "T-Junction" temperature (usually 100°C), it drops its clock speed significantly to cool down.

The Forensic Fix:At Carrillo Tech Solutions, we don't just recommend cooling pads. We perform surgical-level hardware interventions:

* **Repasting:** Replacing factory-grade thermal interface material (TIM) with high-conductivity liquid metal or Kryonaut.
* **Undervolting:** Using software like Throttlestop to lower the voltage sent to the CPU, reducing heat without sacrificing a single frame of performance.
* **Airflow Optimization:** Re-routing internal cables and cleaning specialized fan intakes that factory designs often overlook.

Don't let your hardware lie to you. If you aren't hitting your advertised boost clocks, your system is crying for a thermal overhaul.
